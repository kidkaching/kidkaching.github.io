How It Works

Kids Set Goals

What is it you really want? A new phone? Prom tickets? More money than your younger brother? We keep your money safe so you can focus on earning, saving, and spending on cool things.

Parents Pay Allowance

Set up allowance payments for each of your children using your checking account or credit card. We deposit funds directly in their KaChing account unless you say they didn't earn it.

Kids Earn & Save

No more cash burning a hole in your child's pocket. Funds in KaChing accounts can be trasfered directly to your child's savings account, or back to the parent in exchange for cash.
